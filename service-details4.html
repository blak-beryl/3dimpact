Team Development & Management

We recruit, train, deploy and/or manage Sales Teams for some of our Clients.

We offer 4 mutually exclusive, but collectively exhaustive Salesforce Management and Effectiveness Solutions.

RECRUITMENT:

We have recruited more than 3,000 Salesforce and Marketing members for our clients.
We keep a rich data base of Talents, such that within very short turn around cycles, we are able to get the right talent for our clients


BODY SHOPPING AND HR OPERATIONS:

We provide Body Shopping Solutions for some of our clients. We manage all the HR responsibilities for the Salesforce including Recruitment, Training, Payroll and Industrial Relations


TRAINING & COACHING:

We provide, Basic, Intermediate, Senior and Executive/Strategic Sales and Marketing Coaching and development. We have trained more than 5,000 Sales and Marketing Professionals. Beyond training, and where the brief allows, we support with in-field /On the Job Coaching


SALESFORCE MANAGEMENT:

Some of our clients have trusted us with the turnkey management of their salesforce. We recruit, train, deploy and manage the Salesforce and have responsibility for all field sales and Marketing KPIs.
