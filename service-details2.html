Products Innovation & Renovation

Our Research has shown that more than 90% of Clients have neither an effective Route to Market.

Most executed RTMs do not:
1. Drive Secondary Sales (strong emphasis on Primary Sales to Key Distributors)
2. Give the Brand a line of sight to the Shopper

Our Practice support our clients to develop, implement, monitor and refine their RTM. Our aim is to help our clients
• Maximise Sales with Existing Customers
• Win in White Spaces/Unserved territories
• Drive significant Efficiencies

We support our clients with:

1. Every Dealer Survey (EDS)/All Outlets Census (AOC):

We use Maps to delineate territories and assign Enumerators to “walk the streets” and identify and survey every relevant Retail Outlet… which helps to create a full data base of Retail Customers
Outlet details are obtained include Outlet Type, Physical Address, GPS coordinates, Picture etc.
We also obtain information on how the Outlet is supplied, competitor activity and product categories.

It helps us to:
• Identify different outlet types and quantify number of outlets by type.
• Identify and size potential in unexplored market areas.
• Have a full and up-to-date database of outlets
• Give you a good picture of the Market in terms of Product Availability Competition, Pricing, Market Shares.

2. Route Distribution System/Beat Distribution System Planning/Field Distribution System Planning and Execution.

• Use EDS output or existing data of partners to divide and delineate your route.
• Territory Mapping
• Beat/Route Planning
• Optimising Service/Call Frequency
• Automated Route Planning & Management

3. Last Mile (Sales & Distribution:

The Bane of most products is that they are not able to predictably and consistently get to the Retail Outlets where the Shoppers are. We will help you • Drive distribution, availability & Sales deeper through the Line
• Extend distribution to reach all your Channel Partners
• Eliminate Product Out of Stock at Wholesale and Retail Outlet levels.

4. Channel Classification & Role Definition:

Brands need to adapt their RTM to the Role of the Channel. We support our Clients to define, classify and structure their Channels within very well defined Channel Roles.
